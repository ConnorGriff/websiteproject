3.1: I think in terms of efficiency, yes I could have been much more efficient in my workflow. For example, I wrote all this in Google Docs, and then pasted and formatted it into VS Code. Teamwork again I don’t think would have improved overall productivity however there are ways it could have, it would make dividing up the writing work easier, and perhaps if the team came to an easy compromise over the design and layout of the website then dividing up the workflow would be much simpler.

There was a similar process with the CSS, having the design template or layout I had in mind, then making changes in order to make it line up with what I wanted, and just refreshing and changing as I went along in order for it to meet my vision.
